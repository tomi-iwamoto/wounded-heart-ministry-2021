About Us

Over the years of doing evangelism in New York City, we have met people from all over the world. There are many ways to evangelize- there is personal evangelism, which is a one on one encounter with people telling them about Christ. There is friendship evangelism which is developing relationships with people and bringing them to a church service. One of the most popular is giving away tracts to someone as they pass by.

What we do is called "prayer station evangelism" which incorporates all these aspects. It’s personal, it’s friendship, and it’s also giving away tracts and many times new testaments.

In recent years, we have also been doing a lot of preaching and teaching with Friends of God Ministry, whose founder is Nick Vacaro. This is a church service that is held in a diner in Brooklyn, NY. It is amazing that we are allowed to hold a service in a diner with hundreds of people. Please feel free to look at the gallery page were you will see us fellowshipping together.

In His Grace,
